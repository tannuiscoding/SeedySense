User Guide

Learn how to use SeedySense effectively for the best results.

Gathering Soil Data

To get accurate predictions, you'll need to provide the following soil and environmental data:

  • Nitrogen (N): 0-140 kg/ha
  • Phosphorus (P): 0-140 kg/ha
  • Potassium (K): 0-200 kg/ha
  • Temperature: 0-50Â°C
  • Humidity: 0-100%
  • pH Level: 0-14
  • Rainfall: 0-300 mm

Making Predictions

  1. Navigate to the Prediction page
  2. Enter your soil and environmental data
  3. Click "Predict Crop" to get recommendations
  4. Review the suggested crop and its suitability

Understanding Results

The prediction result shows the most suitable crop based on your input parameters. Consider these factors:

  • Seasonal variations may affect crop suitability
  • Local climate patterns should be considered
  • Results are based on historical agricultural data
